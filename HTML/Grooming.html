Grooming may not seem like a huge part of owning a dog but it is a big time commitment if done right. For bigger dogs who shed they will need to be brushed at least once a day if not more depending on how thick their coat is. To control the shedding you should do weekly or bi-weekly baths with a non-shed shampoo. Some people may not think you need to give your dog a haircut if it sheds but that's simply not true. Giving your dog a haircut will increase their appearance and decrease their shed.
